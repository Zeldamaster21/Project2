Dropping 163 lbs: Daley Plaza

This groundbreaking performance by Industry Of The Ordinary in 2003 involved approximately 75 performers dropping 163 pounds of white clothing onto Daley Plaza in Chicago, symbolizing the average weight of an American adult at the time.
